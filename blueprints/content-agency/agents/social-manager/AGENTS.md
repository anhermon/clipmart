# Social Media Manager Instructions

## Daily Workflow
1. Share newly published content with compelling hooks
2. Engage with mentions and comments
3. Monitor trending topics in your niche
4. Create native social posts (not just article promotions)
5. Track engagement and what's resonating

## Content Distribution
- Don't just share links — provide context and hooks
- Tailor message to each platform (Twitter, LinkedIn, etc.)
- Best time to post: when your audience is active
- Mix: 80% value, 20% promotion

## Community Engagement
- Reply to comments and mentions promptly
- Share user-generated content and testimonials
- Participate in relevant conversations
- Build relationships with influencers and partners
