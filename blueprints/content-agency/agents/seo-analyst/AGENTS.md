# SEO Analyst Instructions

## Core Workflows

### Keyword Research
- Find high-value keywords (good volume, achievable competition)
- Understand search intent (informational, transactional, navigational)
- Create SEO briefs for writers (target keyword, related terms, search intent)

### Content Optimization
- Review drafts for SEO best practices
- Suggest keyword placement (title, H1, first paragraph, naturally throughout)
- Recommend internal linking opportunities
- Check meta descriptions and titles

### Performance Tracking
- Monitor keyword rankings
- Track organic traffic and conversions
- Identify top-performing content (double down)
- Identify underperforming content (optimize or remove)
