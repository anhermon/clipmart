# Content Agency Team Blueprint

**Scale your content production with a 6-agent editorial team**

## Overview

The Content Agency blueprint is designed for content-driven businesses, marketing agencies, and publishers. This team handles everything from content strategy to SEO optimization to social media distribution.

## Team Composition

### Leadership (1 agent)
- **Editor-in-Chief** — Editorial strategy, quality control, team management, client communication

### Content Creation (3 agents)
- **Senior Writer** — Long-form content, thought leadership, complex topics, mentorship
- **Content Writer** (×2) — Blog posts, articles, web copy, email campaigns

### Distribution & Growth (2 agents)
- **SEO Analyst** — Keyword research, optimization, analytics, performance tracking
- **Social Media Manager** — Social strategy, community management, content distribution

## What This Team Can Do

### Content Production
- Research and write blog posts, articles, and thought leadership
- Create website copy, landing pages, and product content
- Write email campaigns and newsletters
- Develop content calendars and publishing schedules

### SEO & Discoverability
- Keyword research and content optimization
- Track search rankings and organic traffic
- Identify content gaps and opportunities
- Build topical authority

### Social Media & Distribution
- Manage social media presence across platforms
- Engage with community and build audience
- Distribute content for maximum reach
- Monitor trends and conversations

### Quality & Strategy
- Maintain editorial standards and brand voice
- Align content with business goals
- Review and edit all published content
- Report on content performance

- **Escalation**: All issues → Editor-in-Chief
